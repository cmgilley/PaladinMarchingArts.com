We believe that performers deserve to have great equipment at a great price. We carefully build all of our creations while thinking about the performers that will use them and the people behind the scenes that will maintain them.

Synth and Mixer Carts

Our carts are designed to feature the performer while making moving onto the field as easy as possible. The carts are at the perfect height to simultaneously show off the performer and hold the equipment they’re using. The 8-inch casters are set as far apart as possible to add stability to the cart and provide a “bumper” when loaded onto a truck.

Learn more

Drumset Carts

Our drum set carts are built to easily hold any standard drum set with an additional space for a throne. The cart rolls on 8-inch casters, and around the cart is a structure of 1 1/2-inch square tube for additional instruments to be clamped onto. We understand that storing such a massive cart can be challenging, so the throne space can fold into the cart and the square tube frames can be disassembled to transform the cart into a flat rolling platform.

Learn more

Racks

Our racks are constructed of our standard 1 ½-inch square tube and 8-inch casters. Standard clamps work perfectly with our design! There is ample space for attaching cymbals, crotales, and drums without them interfering with each other.

Props

All of our props are custom designed and made to fit your show’s needs! Whether your props are for indoor or outdoor use, we’ll hold them to our Paladin standards and make sure they keep your performers safe while looking great.
